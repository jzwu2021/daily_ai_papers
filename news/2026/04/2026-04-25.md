# AI News Digest — 2026-04-25
Curated from recent live sources.

## Overview
The strongest AI news this cycle shows the industry pushing agentic systems into real operating environments: better frontier models, direct app connectivity, deeper enterprise rollouts, and tighter integration with everyday work software. At the same time, labs and platforms are pairing capability upgrades with safety signals and large infrastructure commitments, indicating that adoption is now as much about deployability and governance as raw model quality. The overall pattern is a market moving from isolated model announcements toward full-stack AI execution.
