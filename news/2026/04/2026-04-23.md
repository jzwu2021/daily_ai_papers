### 3. Introducing OpenAI Privacy Filter
Date: 2026-04-22
Source: OpenAI
URL: https://openai.com/index/introducing-openai-privacy-filter
Summary: OpenAI released Privacy Filter, an open-weight model for detecting and redacting personally identifiable information in text. URL: https://www.theverge.com/tech/917163/google-says-75-percent-of-all-its-new-code-is-ai-generated
