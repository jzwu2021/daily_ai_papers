# AI News Digest — 2026-04-21
Curated from recent live sources.

## Overview
The past few days in AI were defined by a split between capability expansion and operational constraint. Frontier labs kept shipping more productized systems for design, coding, and cyber workflows, while the ecosystem around them highlighted the real-world bottlenecks of compute, memory, and business focus. The result is a market that is simultaneously accelerating at the application layer and becoming more disciplined about where capital, talent, and infrastructure get deployed.

## Themes
- Frontier labs are packaging models into domain-specific products rather than relying on generic chat alone.
- AI competition is increasingly about enterprise distribution, monetizable workflows, and public trust.
- Infrastructure remains a hard constraint, with chips and memory shaping what can scale in practice.
- Generative AI is expanding the software creation market instead of cleanly displacing traditional apps.
