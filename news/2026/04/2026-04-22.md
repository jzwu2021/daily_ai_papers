# AI News Digest — 2026-04-22
Curated from recent live sources.

## Overview
The latest AI news shows the market converging on enterprise deployment, trust infrastructure, and supply-chain constraints rather than just raw model announcements. Labs and platforms are pushing AI deeper into software development, workplace rollout, browser experiences, and identity protection, while cloud spending and memory shortages highlight how expensive the next wave of adoption will be. The result is an ecosystem where productization and infrastructure discipline are becoming as important as frontier model capability.

## Themes
- Enterprise AI is shifting from experimentation to scaled deployment in coding, operations, and employee tooling.
- Trust and safety tooling for deepfakes and cyber use cases are becoming product categories of their own.
