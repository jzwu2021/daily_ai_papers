# AI News Digest — 2026-04-24
Curated from recent live sources.

## Overview
The latest AI news shows the market moving from standalone model releases toward operational deployment inside real teams, regulated industries, and office software. OpenAI is shipping more of the agent, privacy, and enterprise scaffolding around its core models, while Google, Microsoft, and NVIDIA are pushing AI deeper into software creation, productivity workflows, and cloud infrastructure. The common thread is that AI competition is increasingly about durable workflow integration and production readiness, not just benchmark leadership.
