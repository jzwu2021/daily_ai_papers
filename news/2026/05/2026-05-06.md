# AI News Digest — 2026-05-06

## Overview
The past two days of AI news show the market moving from model bragging rights toward workflow capture, enterprise distribution, and pre-release governance. Major labs are packaging agents into domain-specific operating systems for finance, while platform players and regulators are formalizing how powerful models get reviewed, secured, and deployed. The bigger pattern is that frontier AI competition increasingly hinges on trust, integration, and delivery capacity rather than standalone model quality alone.
