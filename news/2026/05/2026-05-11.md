# AI News Digest — 2026-05-11

Curated from recent live sources.

## Overview
The latest AI news centers less on one dramatic model launch and more on how leading labs are turning frontier capabilities into dependable products. OpenAI emphasized safe coding agents, cyber-specific access controls, and production voice models, while Anthropic and NVIDIA focused on enterprise workflows and the compute/networking stack that keeps advanced systems usable at scale. The through-line is clear: product reliability, domain fit, and infrastructure control are becoming as important as headline benchmark gains.

### 4. Higher usage limits for Claude and a compute deal with SpaceX
Date: 2026-05-06
Source: Anthropic
URL: https://www.anthropic.com/news/higher-limits-spacex
Summary: Anthropic said it is doubling Claude Code five-hour rate limits for paid plans, removing some peak-hour reductions, and raising Opus API limits while adding more than 300 megawatts of new capacity through a SpaceX agreement. Source: NVIDIA

## Themes
- Safe deployment controls are becoming a competitive differentiator for coding and cyber agents.

- Compute capacity and networking quality now directly shape product limits, reliability, and user experience.
