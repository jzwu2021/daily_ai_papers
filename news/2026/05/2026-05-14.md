## Overview
The latest AI news shows the stack maturing from flashy demos into production systems, enterprise rollout paths, and interface-level changes that affect everyday work. OpenAI focused on making coding agents safer and more deployable inside real organizations, NVIDIA pushed both enterprise agent infrastructure and reinforcement-learning systems, and large consumer platforms kept embedding AI deeper into browsers and shopping surfaces. The common theme is operationalization: trust boundaries, workflow integration, and specialized infrastructure are becoming the real battleground.

## Themes
- Coding agents are being productized through stronger sandboxing, workflow controls, and enterprise deployment support.
- Enterprise agent adoption is moving toward governed, domain-specific systems embedded in existing business software.
- Reinforcement-learning infrastructure is becoming more important as labs look for post-training and self-improvement gains.
- Consumer AI is being woven directly into browsers and commerce interfaces rather than kept as a standalone chatbot.
