# AI News Digest — 2026-05-07
Curated from recent live sources.

## Overview
The past two days of AI news point to a market that is becoming more operational, enterprise-specific, and infrastructure-aware. Labs are shipping broader default-model improvements and domain-targeted agent stacks, while platform players are racing to make agent deployment, networking, and cross-application workflows more practical at scale. The big theme is that competitive edge is shifting from headline model quality alone toward deployability inside real business systems.
